Auystyr is a revolutionary online bookcrossing social website designed exclusively for the university community (SDU).

Our main mission is to connect people through books.

Breathe new life into books! Instead of letting your old favorites gather dust, pass them on to other readers.

Save money

Today, books and knowledge are valuable but costly. Save on new purchases with our book exchange, preserving your savings.

Save time

Using our website you can effectively exchange books via chat and thus you can save your time.

Meet new people

By exchanging books, you can find new friends or the love of your life. Life and our website give you hope for a happy life.
